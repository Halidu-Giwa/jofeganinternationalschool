E-Learning Section
Access to educational, instructional and learning materials and interaction with teachers was made easy in and out of school through our E-learning platforms. The lockdown experienced during the COVID-19 pandemic made the E-learning system seamless with Zoom meetings with teachers and students and online learning with students. Indeed, the dangers of lockdown during COVID-19 was mitigated against. Keeping the children academically busy in and out of school promotes academic excellence and also takes the students out of mischief during holidays and any form of lockdown.
