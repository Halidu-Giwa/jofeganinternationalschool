WHO WE ARE

Jofegan International School, Bida in Niger State, was established in 2008. It has grown during the few Years of its existence to becoming one of the best and Leading schools in Niger State. It is an ICT driven coeducational institution of distinction with Day and Boarding facilities. We strive at all times to prepare our students for a purposeful adult life through the provision of a holistic but balanced education that emphasises the physical, emotional and spiritual development of the child. Education. To achieve this, we give our students/pupils educational experience of unparalleled scope and depth. This is achieved through our various programs – Day Care (Crèche), Nursery, Primary, Secondary and Remedial Studies.

WHY CHOOSE US

Jofegan International School is the first private school at Bida with boarding facilities. It was established as a computer-based co-educational academic institution of distinction where students receive a holistic education that supports their academic, moral, social, physical, technical and spiritual development to enable them meet the challenges of adult life with confidence. Our desire is for our students to be able to compete favorably with their peers both locally and internationally.
